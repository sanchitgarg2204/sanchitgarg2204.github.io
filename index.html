MY TOP 3 PLACES IN IIT KGP

Sanchit Garg

Associate at Communiqué

BASKETBALL COURTS

Majority of my free time is spent here trying to get better at basketball. Also made several friends with a similar love for the game. Courts and rings are really well maintained. Click the photo to get started on your basketball journey.

PAN LOOP FOOD CENTRE

PFC is one of the best places to eat in the IIT Kharagpur campus. It is the only place where you can get decent food. Hope the Secys treat us here xD. Hopefully, one day. Click the photo if you're hungry and lazy.

NEHRU MUSEUM

Nehru museum is a great place to just chill with friends, and the lights make the vibes even better. Inside the museum is also cool stuff, if one wants to check that out. Click the photo if you don't know where it is xD.
